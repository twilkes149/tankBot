Keyboard controls (tankdrive):

ARM Controls Z - turret ccw
X - turret cw
A - Shoulder down
S - Shoulder up
Q - Elbow down
W - Elbow up
C - Wrist down
V - Wrist up
D - Claw close
F - Claw Open

DRIVE Controls
I - Left wheel fwd
K - Left wheel rev
O - Right wheel fwd
L - Right wheel rev
